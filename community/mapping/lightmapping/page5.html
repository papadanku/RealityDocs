5. Importing Statics into 3DsMax

  1. Next step is to import your statics into 3DsMax

  2. Go to BF2 > Bf2 Lightmapping and hit the load Staticobjects.con button

  3. Browse to your map’s Staticobjects.con with all your buildings etc in

  4. Wait thile all your statics are imported, time depends on how many objects are in your map and the complexity of those objects

Once that is done, you should have something a little bit like this.

I would recommend at this point that you Save and backup your 3DsMax Scene and restart your computer.
